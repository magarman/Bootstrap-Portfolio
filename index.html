About Me

Hello and welcome! Meet my sweet girl Xuxa, pronounced "Shoe-sha". She's been making a dent on my couch since summer of last year (2018) after we picked each other at a rescue center... it was like love at first sight (or love at first purr?). Anyhow, Xuxa loves sleeping, rubbing her head against shoes and eating. Some of the ridiculous names she'll respond to, include, but aren't limited to: Moochie, Poochie, Shoosh, Shoosh-Moosh, Beach-ball, Shoshanna, Susan, etc. Every morning Xuxa greets me with loud purrs and squinty eyes, her sweet and mellow demeanor make her the most lovely kitty on earth.
